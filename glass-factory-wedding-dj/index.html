About The Glass Factory

Venue Information

Location: 601 N Myrtle Avenue, Jacksonville, FL 32204

Capacity: Up to 500+ guests

Style: Industrial-chic, exposed brick, soaring ceilings

Event Spaces: Main Hall, Mezzanine, Outdoor Courtyard

Website: theglassfactoryjax.com

The Glass Factory is a converted industrial space in Jacksonville's urban core that has become one of the city's most popular wedding venues. The 16,000 square foot facility features exposed brick walls, soaring ceilings with original beams, and the kind of raw character that creates unforgettable photographs. The venue's flexibility allows for everything from intimate gatherings to large-scale celebrations, with couples appreciating the blank canvas that lets them create their own vision.
