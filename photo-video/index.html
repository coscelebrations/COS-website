Questions

Frequently Asked

Can I add photo and video to my existing DJ package?

Absolutely. Many couples book their DJ entertainment first and add photo/video services later. We offer bundled packages that combine DJ, photography, and videography at a better value than booking separately. Contact us to discuss adding photo/video to your existing booking.

How do your photographers and DJs work together?

This is where booking through COS Celebrations really shines. Our DJ and photo/video teams coordinate before and during your wedding to ensure seamless coverage. The DJ knows exactly when to cue the photographer for key moments, and the photographers know the timeline inside and out. No miscommunication, no missed moments.

What is included in wedding photography packages?

Our wedding photography packages include full-day coverage from getting ready through reception, a second shooter for larger weddings, professionally edited high-resolution images, an online gallery for easy sharing, and print rights. Engagement sessions can be added to any package.

When will I receive my photos and video?

Sneak peeks of your best photos are typically delivered within 1-2 weeks of your wedding. Full edited galleries are delivered within 4-6 weeks. Cinematic highlight films are delivered within 6-8 weeks, with full ceremony and reception edits following shortly after.

Do you cover destination weddings outside Northeast Florida?

Yes! While we are based in St. Augustine and primarily serve Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Orlando, and Tampa, we are available for destination weddings throughout Florida and beyond. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our standard service area.

How do I book photo and video services?

Contact us through our website or call to schedule a consultation. We will discuss your vision, wedding details, and create a custom package that fits your needs. A signed contract and retainer secure your date. We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for popular wedding dates.
