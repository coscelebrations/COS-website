Omni Amelia Island Resort stands as one of Florida's most impressive destination wedding venues — an AAA Four Diamond resort offering 80,000 square feet of event space, 3.5 miles of private beach, and seven distinct ceremony sites ranging from oceanfront to marsh-side settings. The 16,000 square foot Amelia Ballroom alone can host up to 1,000 guests, while intimate venues like Walker's Landing offer waterfront charm for smaller celebrations. COS Celebrations is honored to be a preferred vendor at Omni Amelia Island, understanding the seamless coordination required across this expansive resort.
