The Florida Aquarium offers Tampa's most unforgettable wedding setting - a 250,000 square foot AZA-accredited facility in the heart of Channelside where couples exchange vows before a stunning 500,000-gallon coral reef window with over 2,000 fish as witnesses. With three unique event spaces including the Coral Reef Room, Rooftop Terrace with city skyline views, and First Floor Lobby, this venue delivers wedding magic found nowhere else on earth. COS Celebrations understands that couples who choose The Florida Aquarium want their celebration to be as extraordinary as the setting. Our DJ + live saxophone provides entertainment worthy of this underwater wonderland - enchanting during cocktails as sharks glide past, electric when the celebration reaches full swing.
