5 Things to Compare When Hiring a Jacksonville Wedding DJ

01

Ask for Venue-Specific Experience

A DJ who has worked your venue before already knows the room acoustics, power outlet locations, load-in logistics, and noise ordinance cutoff times. At Epping Forest, for example, the ballroom ceiling bounces bass differently than The River Club's open floor plan on the 35th floor. COS Celebrations has performed at every major Jacksonville wedding venue and coordinates directly with venue staff before each event.

02

Read Reviews That Mention Specifics

Five-star ratings are easy to collect from friends and family. Look for reviews that describe specific moments — how the DJ handled a timeline change, kept older guests dancing, or transitioned from dinner to the dance floor. COS has 500+ reviews across The Knot, WeddingWire, and Google, and the majority reference specific songs, venues, or reception moments.

03

Understand What the Price Includes

Some Jacksonville DJs quote low then add fees for ceremony coverage, travel, overtime, or wireless microphones. Ask for an all-in number. COS packages start at $1,500 and include DJ/MC services, a premium sound system, wireless mics, backup equipment, and our planning app — with no travel fees anywhere in Duval County.

04

Check the Planning Process

The best wedding DJs in Jacksonville do the real work before the event. Ask how they build a timeline, whether you can submit song requests and do-not-play lists, and how they coordinate with your photographer and planner. COS provides every couple with our Music + Timeline app months before the wedding, and we schedule planning calls with vendors so the day runs without surprises.

05

Consider Live Music Options

Not every couple needs live musicians, but knowing the option exists matters. Most Jacksonville DJ companies offer DJ-only packages.
