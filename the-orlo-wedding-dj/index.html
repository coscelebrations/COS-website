The Orlo stands as one of Tampa's most storied wedding venues — a magnificent 1896 mansion built for Orlo J. Spafford, president of Tampa National Bank, with an elegant 1922 ballroom added by the Tampa Women's Club. Located in the heart of historic Hyde Park, this private social club exudes the refined elegance of a bygone era. COS Celebrations understands that couples who choose The Orlo are drawn to its rich history and sophisticated atmosphere. Our DJ + live saxophone provides entertainment that respects the mansion's heritage — refined during cocktails in the historic parlors, captivating when the ballroom's four chandeliers illuminate your first dance.
