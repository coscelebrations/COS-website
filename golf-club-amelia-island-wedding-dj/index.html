Golf Club of Amelia Island offers one of Florida's most scenic wedding settings — an award-winning Mark McCumber and Gene Littler designed course with oceanfront views and the option for beach ceremonies just steps from the clubhouse. With capacity for up to 250 guests and both indoor and outdoor event spaces, this venue combines the elegance of a private golf club with the natural beauty of Amelia Island's coastline. COS Celebrations is honored to be a preferred vendor at Golf Club of Amelia Island, understanding that couples who choose this venue expect sophisticated entertainment that enhances rather than overwhelms the stunning natural setting. Our DJ + live saxophone creates the perfect ambiance for watching the sun set over the 18th fairway before transitioning to an evening of celebration.
