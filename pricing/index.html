Your Questions Answered

Pricing FAQs

Why does pricing vary from $1,500 to $3,000?

The range depends on event length, add-ons you choose (like live saxophone or uplighting), and which DJ you select. Owner Corey Peterson's packages start higher due to demand, while newer team members offer excellent service at lower rates.

What's the most popular add-on?

Live saxophone during cocktail hour and reception is our number one upgrade. Guests consistently say it's their favorite unexpected touch. We also have violinists, guitarists, and other musicians available.

Do you charge travel fees?

Events within 50 miles of St. Augustine have no travel fee. For venues farther out (Orlando, Tampa, Savannah), we add a small travel fee to cover fuel and extra time. We'll include this in your quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), checks, Venmo, and bank transfers. A $500 retainer holds your date, with the balance due 30 days before your event.
