Orlando Wedding DJ FAQ

How much does a wedding DJ cost in Orlando?

The average Orlando wedding runs about $35,000 total - entertainment is typically 3-5% of that. COS packages start at $1,500 for four hours. Most couples at Bella Collina or Paradise Cove land between $2,000 and $2,800 depending on ceremony coverage and add-ons. All-in pricing, no surprises.

Do you actually have a team in Orlando?

Yes. Our dedicated Orlando team are Central Florida locals who perform at Orlando venues every weekend. No travel fees, no one driving in from out of town.

When should I book for an Orlando wedding?

12 to 18 months out, especially for peak season (October through April). Orlando hosts nearly 18,000 weddings a year. Bella Collina and Paradise Cove book almost every weekend. Our team fills up fast during fall and spring.

Can I plan from out of state?

We do this constantly. Orlando is one of America's biggest destination wedding markets - over 74 million visitors a year, which means your guests already want to come. Our planning app handles playlist, timeline, and vendor coordination remotely. You don't need to be here until wedding week.

What about summer storms?

Orlando's rainy season runs May through September. Afternoon thunderstorms are almost daily but typically pass in 30-60 minutes. If your ceremony is outdoors, have a rain plan. We always prep a quick-move sound setup for outdoor Orlando venues.

Is there a more affordable option?

Yes - our sister company AE Entertainment offers DJ-only packages starting at $800 for Orlando weddings. Same team, same planning app, same reliability - just without the live musicians.
