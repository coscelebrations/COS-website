About Bowing Oaks Plantation

Venue Information

Location: Jacksonville, FL

Capacity: Up to 200+ guests

Style: Rustic Outdoor, Historic Oaks, Southern Charm

Event Spaces: Oak Canopy Ceremony Site, Reception Pavilion, Bridal Suite

Website: bowingoaks.com

Bowing Oaks Plantation takes its name from the magnificent live oak trees that seem to bow in reverence, their massive branches creating natural arches perfect for wedding ceremonies. This romantic outdoor venue captures the essence of Southern hospitality and natural beauty, offering couples a wedding setting that requires little decoration — nature has already provided the most stunning backdrop imaginable. The combination of historic oaks, Spanish moss, and open-air celebration creates an unforgettable experience for couples and guests alike.
