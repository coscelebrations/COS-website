Common Questions

Booking FAQs

How far in advance should I book?

Popular dates book 12-18 months in advance, especially for Saturday weddings in peak season (October, November, and spring months). We recommend reaching out as soon as you have your venue and date confirmed.

Can I request a specific DJ?

Absolutely! Many couples come to us because they've seen a specific DJ at a friend's wedding or been referred. Just let us know who you'd like to work with in the form above, and we'll check their availability for your date.

Do you require a deposit to hold my date?

Yes, a $500 retainer secures your date and DJ. We hold dates for 7 days after sending a quote, but popular dates can be requested by multiple couples. The remaining balance is due 30 days before your event.

What if my venue isn't on your list?

No problem! We've performed at over 200 venues across Florida and Georgia. If your venue isn't in our dropdown, simply type the name and city in the fields provided. We're always excited to work at new locations.
