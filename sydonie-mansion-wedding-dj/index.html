Sydonie Mansion stands as one of Central Florida's most extraordinary wedding destinations — a 22,000+ square foot Mediterranean Revival masterpiece with 42 rooms, designed after the Alhambra Castle in Spain by renowned architect Grosvenor Atterbury. Originally the winter retreat of steel baron James Laughlin, this historic lakefront estate on Lake Minore offers an atmosphere of old-world grandeur that few venues in Florida can match. COS Celebrations understands that couples who discover Sydonie Mansion are seeking something truly exceptional. Our DJ + live saxophone provides entertainment worthy of this architectural treasure — sophisticated during cocktails in the courtyards, unforgettable when the celebration fills those magnificent rooms.
