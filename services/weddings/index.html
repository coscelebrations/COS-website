How It Works

The COS Wedding Process

01

Introduction

A direct meeting with your COS DJ to talk about your wedding plans, vision, vibes, and services. Schedule a time that works for you, using Zoom, phone, or FaceTime.

02

Dream

With COS, there's no pushy sales. After our meeting, we provide you with all the information needed to make the best decision for your big day. Whatever the vibe, our team is dedicated to inspiring you to have the wedding you deserve.

03

Planning

When you're ready to move forward, we begin the planning process. You'll get your own COS Music & Planning App with your services, the soundtrack to your day, and everything in between.

04

Craft

As your wedding day approaches, you'll fill out your COS Music & Timeline App. We'll dedicate the necessary time to ensure that your wedding plans are brought to life and schedule our final meetings.
