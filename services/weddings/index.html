Your Questions Answered

Wedding DJ FAQs

Why should I choose a DJ who also plays live instruments?

A DJ who plays live instruments brings a unique energy that recorded music simply cannot match. When our saxophonist walks onto the dance floor playing alongside your favorite songs, guests react viscerally-it creates moments that people talk about for years. Live musicians also read the room in real-time and can adjust their performance based on the crowd's energy, creating a dynamic experience that keeps the dance floor packed all night.

What happens if my wedding timeline changes day-of?

Timeline changes happen at almost every wedding, and our team is trained to handle them seamlessly. We coordinate directly with your planner, photographer, and venue staff throughout the day. Our COS Music + Timeline app keeps everyone on the same page, and our DJs are experts at reading the room and adjusting on the fly. You'll never notice a hiccup-that's our job.

How do you keep the dance floor full without being cheesy?

We believe in smooth, confident MC work that keeps guests informed without constant interruptions. No growling your names like a boxing announcer. No "1-2-3 everybody say hey!" Instead, we guide the evening with tasteful energy and let the music do the talking. Our DJs study daily with top wedding entertainers across the country to stay sharp and deliver the kind of experience your guests deserve.

Can I customize the music playlist for my wedding?

Absolutely. Our COS Music + Timeline app gives you complete control over your wedding soundtrack. You can request specific songs for key moments like your first dance, parent dances, and bouquet toss. You can also create "must play" and "do not play" lists to ensure we nail the vibe you want. During the reception, our DJs blend your requests with their expert song selection to keep the energy flowing.

What's included in your wedding DJ packages?

Every COS Celebrations wedding package includes professional DJ and MC services, a premium sound system sized for your venue, wireless microphones for toasts and announcements, access to our COS Music + Timeline planning app, pre-wedding consultations, and an elegant DJ setup that blends with your décor. Additional services like live musicians, lighting design, photo booths, and special effects can be added based on your vision and budget.
