Questions

Frequently Asked

What is a hybrid DJ + live musician?

A hybrid DJ + live musician combines the versatility of a professional DJ with the energy of live performance. Instead of just playing tracks, our saxophonist, pianist, or drummer performs live alongside the DJ during key moments of your reception. This creates an immersive, concert-like experience while maintaining the song variety only a DJ can provide.

When do the live musicians perform during the wedding?

Live musicians typically perform during cocktail hour (ambient sets), the first dance or special dances, and 2-3 high-energy sets during the reception dance party. The saxophone might walk onto the dance floor for a surprise 15-minute set, creating a moment guests talk about for years. We customize timing based on your preferences.

How is this different from hiring a separate band and DJ?

When you hire a separate band and DJ, they rarely collaborate ‚Äî you get two distinct acts. With COS Celebrations, the live musician and DJ are one cohesive team, rehearsing together and performing as a unit. Our founder is both the DJ and saxophonist, so the integration is seamless. You get band energy at a fraction of the cost.

How much does hybrid DJ + live musician cost?

Hybrid entertainment packages at COS Celebrations typically range from $2,000 to $4,500 depending on coverage hours and musician selection. This delivers the energy of a live band at roughly half the cost while maintaining full song versatility. Most couples find the impact-to-investment ratio exceptional.
