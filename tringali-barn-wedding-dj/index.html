Tringali Barn sits on 54 breathtaking acres bordering Fort Matanzas State Park, just 10 miles from downtown St. Augustine. Step onto the property and you'll understand why couples fall in love with this place — it feels like its own world, peaceful and removed from the everyday. The 7,000-square-foot barn features soaring ceilings and rustic wood beams that create an atmosphere both grand and intimate. Outside, a beautiful lake offers stunning reflection shots during golden hour, while the surrounding forest provides dappled natural light that photographers dream about. COS Celebrations brings something special to this setting: our DJ + live saxophone combination transforms the barn's natural acoustics into something magical. Whether your guests are dancing under those soaring ceilings, gathering around the fire pit after dark, or enjoying cocktails as the sun sets over the lake, we ensure every moment has the perfect soundtrack. With capacity for up to 300 guests and no travel fees from St. Augustine, Tringali Barn is exactly the kind of venue we love to work.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a wedding DJ cost at Tringali Barn?

Wedding DJ packages at Tringali Barn start at $1,500 for 4 hours of professional DJ and MC services. Most couples at this venue invest between $1,800 and $2,500 to add ceremony coverage, additional hours, or live saxophone.

Can you handle outdoor ceremonies at Tringali Barn?

Absolutely. We bring battery-powered ceremony systems perfect for Tringali Barn's outdoor spaces. Whether you're exchanging vows under the oaks or by the lake, we ensure every guest hears your ceremony clearly.

What makes COS a good choice for a barn wedding?

Barn venues have unique acoustics — high ceilings, wooden surfaces, and open spaces. We've performed at rustic venues throughout Florida and know how to dial in sound that fills the space without overwhelming it. Plus, our live saxophone adds an elegant touch that elevates the barn setting.

Looking for a wedding DJ near me in St. Augustine?

COS Celebrations is based in St. Augustine and serves Tringali Barn and all St. Augustine area venues with no travel fees. We're one of the top-rated wedding entertainment companies in Northeast Florida with over 500 five-star reviews.
