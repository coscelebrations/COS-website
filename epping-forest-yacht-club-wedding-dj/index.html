About Epping Forest Yacht & Country Club

Venue Information

Location: 1830 Epping Forest Drive, Jacksonville, FL 32217

Capacity: Up to 300 guests

Style: 1920s Mediterranean Revival estate, riverfront, private club

Built in 1927 by Alfred I. duPont, Epping Forest was designed as a private estate overlooking the St. Johns River. The Mediterranean Revival architecture, imported Italian tiles, and lush gardens create a setting that feels like stepping back in time—while offering modern amenities for today's celebrations. The venue offers multiple ceremony locations, from the iconic riverfront lawn to the intimate garden spaces, plus a stunning ballroom for receptions. As a private yacht and country club, Epping Forest provides an exclusive experience that larger venues simply can't match.
