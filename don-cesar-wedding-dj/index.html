The Don CeSar is Florida's most iconic beachfront wedding venue — a magnificent 1928 Spanish-style landmark known as the "Pink Palace" and listed on the National Register of Historic Places since 1974. With 38,000 square feet of event space, ten distinct venues, and sunset ceremonies on pristine Gulf beaches with views of the famous beach swing, The Don CeSar offers wedding magic found nowhere else. COS Celebrations understands that couples who choose the Pink Palace are creating an unforgettable experience. Our DJ + live saxophone provides entertainment worthy of this legendary setting — romantic during cocktails on the oceanview terrace, electrifying when the ballroom celebration reaches full swing.
