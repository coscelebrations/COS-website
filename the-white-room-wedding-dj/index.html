Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a wedding DJ cost at The White Room?

COS Celebrations packages for White Room weddings start at $1,500 for professional DJ/MC services. Most couples invest between $2,200 and $3,500 for ceremony coverage, live saxophone during first dance, and premium uplighting that complements the venue's elegant aesthetic.

Are you preferred vendors at The White Room?

Yes, COS Celebrations is a preferred vendor at The White Room with over 100 weddings at this venue. Their team knows us well and trusts our professionalism, which makes coordination seamless for your wedding day.

Can you do ceremonies on the Rooftop?

Absolutely. We've performed dozens of ceremonies on The White Room Rooftop and understand the unique challenges of outdoor sound with downtown views. We bring appropriate wireless microphones and backup equipment for Florida weather.

What makes COS different from other White Room DJs?

We're musicians first — our founder toured internationally as a saxophonist before starting COS. That's why we offer live saxophone during your first dance and key moments, creating magic that speakers can't replicate. Combined with 100+ White Room weddings, we know this venue inside and out.

Do you offer uplighting for The White Room?

Yes, and it's one of our most popular add-ons for White Room weddings. The Grand Ballroom's architecture looks stunning with intelligent LED uplighting, and we've dialed in the perfect colors and placement after hundreds of events in this space.

How far in advance should we book for a White Room wedding?

The White Room is one of St. Augustine's most popular venues, so prime dates book quickly. We recommend securing your DJ 10-14 months in advance, especially for peak season weekends (March-May, September-November).
