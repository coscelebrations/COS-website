The Fountain of Youth Archaeological Park isn't your typical wedding venue — and that's exactly why couples love it. Nestled on 15 acres along the Intracoastal Waterway, this historic St. Augustine landmark offers something truly unique: waterfront ceremonies under ancient oak trees and receptions in a stunning pavilion with panoramic water views. We've performed multiple weddings here and understand the venue's personality — the way sound carries across the water, how to work with the natural acoustics of the pavilion, and the perfect moments to surprise guests with cold sparks and live saxophone. When you book COS Celebrations for your Fountain of Youth wedding, you're getting a team that knows how to make this already-magical venue absolutely unforgettable.

About Fountain of Youth Archaeological Park

Venue Information

Location: 11 Magnolia Ave, St. Augustine, FL 32084

Capacity: Up to 200 guests

Style: Waterfront pavilion, historic grounds, oak tree ceremonies

Website: fountainofyouthflorida.com

Fountain of Youth Archaeological Park offers a wedding experience unlike any other in St. Augustine. The venue's 15 acres along the Intracoastal Waterway include ancient oak trees for ceremonies, a beautiful waterfront pavilion for receptions, and the kind of natural Florida beauty that photographs beautifully. The historic significance of the site — believed to be the landing spot of Ponce de León in 1513 — adds a layer of meaning to your celebration. It's the perfect backdrop for couples who want something memorable, meaningful, and a little bit different.
