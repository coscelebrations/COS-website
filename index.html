Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a wedding DJ cost in Jacksonville and St. Augustine?

COS Celebrations wedding DJ packages start at $1,500 and most couples invest between $1,500 and $3,000 depending on hours of coverage and add-on services like live saxophone, lighting design, and photo booths. Our pricing includes professional DJ and MC services, premium sound equipment, wireless microphones, and access to our COS Music + Timeline planning app.

What makes COS Celebrations different from other wedding DJs?

COS Celebrations is one of the only wedding DJ companies in Florida that offers live musicians who actually perform with the DJ. Our founder toured 35 countries performing with artists like Usher, Alicia Keys, and Big Gigantic before bringing that concert-level experience to weddings. We pioneered the DJ plus live saxophone model in North Florida, and our team trains daily with top wedding DJs across the country.

Do you offer live musicians for weddings?

Yes, live musicians are our specialty and what sets COS Celebrations apart. We offer live saxophone, piano, violin, cello, harp, drums, trumpet, and full fusion bands that perform alongside the DJ during your reception. For ceremonies and cocktail hours, we provide piano, violin, harp, guitar, and string ensembles. Our musicians don't just play background music—they read the crowd and feed off the energy to create unforgettable moments.

What areas do you serve in Florida?

COS Celebrations serves North and Central Florida including Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Ponte Vedra Beach, Amelia Island, Orlando, and Tampa. We are based in St. Augustine and offer no travel fees for Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Ponte Vedra, and Amelia Island. Travel fees may apply for Orlando and Tampa weddings depending on the date and venue.

How far in advance should I book my wedding DJ?

We recommend booking your wedding DJ 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for popular dates like Saturdays in spring and fall. COS Celebrations performs at over 500 events per year and our most requested DJs book up quickly. However, we sometimes have availability for shorter timelines, so don't hesitate to reach out even if your wedding is coming up soon.

What venues are you a preferred vendor at?

COS Celebrations is a preferred vendor at over 50 venues across North Florida including TPC Sawgrass (where we are the top preferred entertainment vendor), Treasury on the Plaza, Lightner Museum, The White Room, River House, Fountain of Youth, Timuquana Country Club, Epping Forest Yacht Club, Ponte Vedra Inn and Club, and Ritz-Carlton Amelia Island. Being a preferred vendor means the venue trusts us to deliver exceptional service for their couples.
