About Treasury on the Plaza

Venue Information

Location: 24 Cathedral Place, St. Augustine, FL 32084

Capacity: Up to 300 guests

Style: Historic bank building, 1920s architecture, downtown St. Augustine

Website: treasuryontheplaza.com

Treasury on the Plaza has been one of St. Augustine's most coveted wedding venues for good reason. The soaring 30-foot ceilings, restored terrazzo floors, and original bank vault create a one-of-a-kind backdrop. The venue offers both indoor elegance and a charming courtyard for ceremonies or cocktail hour, all steps from the historic Plaza de la Constitución. It's the kind of venue that photographs beautifully and feels magical in person — exactly the setting that deserves world-class entertainment.
