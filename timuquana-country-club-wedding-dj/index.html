Timuquana Country Club isn't just another venue on our list - it's one of Jacksonville's most prestigious and historic settings for a wedding. When you book COS Celebrations for your Timuquana wedding, you're getting a team that knows this venue inside and out.

Timuquana Country Club has been one of Jacksonville's most prestigious wedding venues since 1923. The Spanish Colonial Revival architecture, sweeping river views, and manicured grounds with ancient oak trees draped in Spanish moss create a one-of-a-kind backdrop for your celebration. Whether you choose an outdoor ceremony by the water or in the elegant ballroom, Timuquana offers the kind of timeless sophistication that photographs beautifully and feels magical in person - exactly the setting that deserves world-class entertainment.
