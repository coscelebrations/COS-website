Dr. Phillips House stands as downtown Orlando's most distinguished historic wedding venue — an 1893 Victorian mansion on the National Register of Historic Places, originally built as a wedding gift and once home to Dr. Philip Phillips, Florida's first orange juice baron. With capacity for up to 250 guests, lakefront views on Lake Lucerne, and the only remaining Shingle Style architecture in Orlando, this venue offers a rare combination of historic significance and elegant entertaining space. COS Celebrations understands that couples who choose Dr. Phillips House appreciate the weight of history. Our DJ + live saxophone provides sophisticated entertainment that honors the venue's Victorian grandeur while creating unforgettable modern celebrations.
