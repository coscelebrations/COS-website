Kanapaha Botanical Gardens is Gainesville's most spectacular natural wedding venue — a 68-acre non-profit botanical facility featuring 24 major plant collections, the largest herb garden in the Southeast, and Florida's most impressive bamboo display. Connected by 1.5 miles of paved walkways, couples can choose from numerous enchanting ceremony sites scattered throughout the gardens. COS Celebrations understands that couples who choose Kanapaha are drawn to nature's unmatched beauty. Our DJ + live saxophone provides entertainment that enhances the natural setting — ethereal during garden ceremonies, magical when the celebration continues as fireflies emerge at dusk.
